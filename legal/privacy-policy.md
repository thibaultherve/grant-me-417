Get Granted ("we", "us", "our") is a free web application operated by Thibault Hervé, an individual based in France. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal data when you use our service at getgranted.com and app.getgranted.com (the "Service").

## 3. Data We Collect

### 3.1 Account Data

When you create an account, we collect:

- **Email address** — to identify your account and communicate with you
- **Password** — stored as a cryptographic hash (bcrypt); we never store or access your plain-text password
- **First name and last name** (optional) — for display purposes only

### 3.2 Profile Data

To provide visa eligibility features, we collect:

- **Nationality** (country code) — to determine WHV 417 visa eligibility rules
- **UK citizen exemption status** — to apply specific visa rules

### 3.3 Visa Data

To track your visa progress, we collect:

- **Visa type** (1st, 2nd, or 3rd Working Holiday Visa)
- **Arrival date in Australia**
- **Days required, days worked, and progress** — calculated by the Service

### 3.4 Employer Data

To track work eligibility, we collect:

- **Employer name**
- **Industry type** (e.g., farming, mining, construction)
- **Employer location** (Australian suburb and postcode)
- **Eligibility status** for WHV 417 specified work

### 3.5 Work Entry Data

To calculate your progress, we collect:

- **Dates worked**
- **Hours worked per day**
- **Associated employer**

### 3.6 Technical Data

We automatically collect:

- **Authentication tokens** — to keep you securely logged in (stored as hashed values)
- **Server logs** — IP address, browser type, and access timestamps (retained for security purposes)

## 6. Data Storage and Security

- **Location:** Your data is stored on servers in Helsinki, Finland (European Union), operated by Railway.
- **Encryption:** All data is encrypted in transit (TLS/HTTPS) and at rest.
- **Passwords:** Stored using bcrypt hashing — we cannot read your password.
- **Authentication:** JWT-based with short-lived access tokens (15 minutes) and refresh token rotation.
- **Access:** Only the Data Controller (Thibault Hervé) has access to the database. No employees or third parties have access.

## 7. Data Retention

| Data | Retention Period |
|---|---|
| Account data | Until you delete your account |
| Visa, employer, and work entry data | Until you delete your account |
| Authentication tokens | Automatically expired (7 days for refresh tokens) |
| Server logs | 90 days |

When you delete your account, all your personal data is permanently deleted from our database (cascade deletion). This action is irreversible.

## 8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you have the following rights:

### Under GDPR (EU/EEA residents)

- **Right of access** — request a copy of your personal data
- **Right to rectification** — correct inaccurate data
- **Right to erasure** ("right to be forgotten") — request deletion of your data
- **Right to data portability** — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
- **Right to restrict processing** — limit how we use your data
- **Right to object** — object to processing based on legitimate interest
- **Right to withdraw consent** — where processing is based on consent
- **Right to lodge a complaint** — with the CNIL (France) or your local data protection authority

### Under Australian Privacy Act

- **Right of access** — request access to your personal information
- **Right to correction** — request correction of inaccurate data
- **Right to complain** — to the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC)

### How to Exercise Your Rights

Contact us at **privacy@getgranted.com**. We will respond within 30 days (or as required by applicable law).

You can also delete your account directly from the Service, which permanently removes all your data.
